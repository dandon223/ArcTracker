Game menu
=========

.. spec:: New chapter
    :id: SPEC_CARD_COUNTING_1
    :status: open

    ArcTracker shall have `new chapter` button that allows user to move active game to new round.
    This will be allowed during game. After new chapter round counter goes to zero.

.. spec:: New round
    :id: SPEC_CARD_COUNTING_2
    :status: open

    ArcTracker shall have `new round` button that allows user to move active game to new round in chapter.

.. spec:: Mark played cards
    :id: SPEC_CARD_COUNTING_3
    :status: open

    ArcTracker shall allow user to add one `face up` card and one `face down` card or one `face down` card or two `face down` cards to player name during one round.

.. spec:: Mark retrieved card
    :id: SPEC_CARD_COUNTING_4
    :status: open

    ArcTracker shall allow user to retrieve card to player during round.

.. spec:: Mark added cards
    :id: SPEC_CARD_COUNTING_5
    :status: open

    ArcTracker shall allow user to add or substract number of `face down` cards to player hand during round.

.. spec:: Mark revealed card
    :id: SPEC_CARD_COUNTING_6
    :status: open

    ArcTracker shall allow user to reveal card from player hand during round.

.. spec:: Mark unrevealed card
    :id: SPEC_CARD_COUNTING_7
    :status: open

    ArcTracker shall allow user to unrevealed card to player hand during round.

.. spec:: Show card hands
    :id: SPEC_CARD_COUNTING_8
    :status: open

    ArcTracker shall present to user current player hands during game.

.. spec:: Show cards played
    :id: SPEC_CARD_COUNTING_9
    :status: open

    ArcTracker shall present to user cards played during current chapter.

.. spec:: Show cards in play
    :id: SPEC_CARD_COUNTING_10
    :status: open

    ArcTracker shall present to user cards that are believed to be available to play during current round.
